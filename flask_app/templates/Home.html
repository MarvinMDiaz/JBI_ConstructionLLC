Our Services

Expert Construction & Remodeling in Northern Virginia

At JBI Construction LLC, we specialize in Residential and Commercial Construction, remodeling,renovations across Chantilly, Fairfax, and Northern Virginia. From project planning and design to building and finishing work, our expert team delivers high-quality craftsmanship on time and within budget.

Kitchen Remodeling

Our kitchen remodeling service includes a full design and build process to transform your outdated kitchen into a functional and stylish space that meets your unique needs and tastes.

Bathroom Remodeling

Our bathroom remodeling services include design, construction, and finishing touches to create a customized, and aesthetically pleasing space. We handle all aspects of the process and aim for the highest quality workmanship for our customers.

Painting

Our painting services include both interior and exterior painting for residential and commercial properties. We offer a wide range of color options and use high quality paints and techniques to ensure a long-lasting and flawless finish.

Carpentry

We offer comprehensive carpentry services, including custom projects such as cabinets, shelving, trim, and molding. Our skilled craftsmen deliver exceptional quality and attention to detail.

Decks & Patios

We offer custom deck and patio building services, including design and construction. Our experienced team handles all aspects of the project and delivers high quality results.

Flooring Installation

We offer professional floor installation services for a variety of materials including hardwood, laminate, tile, and carpet. Our experienced team ensures a smooth, even finish and works with clients to choose the best option for their needs and budget.
